Overview

Coffee is a popular beverage drank around the world. It's primarily known to be used for boosting energy, but there are many alternative usages too. It can be used for socialization, health benefits, and taste!

Coffee is the second most popular drink in the world, just behind water. However, 66% of Americans consume coffee daily which is even more than the percentage of bottled water that falls around 64%. Each year, approximately 400 billion cups are consumed. This is ~5.5 billion pounds of coffee beans!

Just in America, about 400 million cups of coffee are consumed on the daily, which averages around 3 cups per person. 2 out of 3 Americans brew coffee at home every day! Within the past year coffee prices have risen around 20%!
